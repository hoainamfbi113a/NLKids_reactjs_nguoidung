    Bản dịch của see
    động từ
    Tần suất

    Cho biết tần suất một bản dịch xuất hiện trong các tài liệu công khai

    xem
    see, look, refer, reckon, contemplate, consult
    thấy
    see, seen, look, behold, spy
    xem xét
    consider, examine, see, inspect, investigate, observe
    nhìn thấy
    see, behold, descry
    nhận ra
    notice, see, spot, know, perceive, distinguish
    trông thấy
    see, sight, behold, view
    lưu ý
    mind, heed, regard, see
    7 bản dịch khác
    Nghĩa của see
    động từ
    1
    perceive with the eyes; discern visually.
    It worries me that people living in those flats could see into the bedrooms.
    từ đồng nghĩa: discern spot notice catch sight of glimpse catch/get a glimpse of make out pick out spy distinguish detect perceive note lay/set eyes on behold descry espy
    2
    discern or deduce mentally after reflection or from information; understand.
    With the benefit of hindsight, we can see that the disruption was permanent.
    3
    meet (someone one knows) socially or by chance.
    I went to see Caroline
    từ đồng nghĩa: encounter meet run into/across come across stumble on/across happen on chance on bump into meet meet up with get together with socialize with
    4
    escort or conduct (someone) to a specified place.
    don't bother seeing me out
    từ đồng nghĩa: escort accompany show walk conduct lead take usher attend
    5
    (in poker or brag) equal the bet of (an opponent).
    If the discarded cards were also equal in rank then the player who was seen wins the tie.
    2 nghĩa khác
